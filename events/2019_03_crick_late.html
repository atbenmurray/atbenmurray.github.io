Crick Late

Workshop 'Binding Art & Science, a journey through Drug Design'

You are invited to take part in constructing a giant protein strand sculpture that will weave through and around lab equipment commonly in use at the Crick. Explore how scientists design microscopic ligands that fit perfectly into complex protein shapes. Workshop developed in collaboration with Christelle Soudy (Senior Laboratory Research Scientist at the Crick), and Beatrice Haines (Artist).

Francis Crick Institute, 1 Midland Road, London NW1 1AT.

13th March 2019, from 18:00 - 21.30pm

Further details on the Crick website.
