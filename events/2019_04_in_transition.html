We are proud to announce that we will be part of an upcoming collective exhibition at the Ugly Duck, in London.

We have been to see exhibitions at this great venue on many occasions now, and we are very happy to be a part of an exhibition there!

Find further details about the exhibition at their website. The exhibition is at 49 Tanner St. and is on from the April 11th to April 14th, inclusive; we look forward to seeing you there!
