Anamorphic Waves

Anamorphic Waves is a collective exhibition exploring how digital interfaces and technological tools are reshaping our personal, professional and ecological relationships.

Come to interact with Biomorpha (Evolving Structures), our digital interactive installation about mutation and evolutionary pressure.

Ugly Duck, 49 Tanner St. London SE1 3PL

From 12th to 14th April 2019

Private View: 11th April 2019 at 18:00h. Free tickets on Eventbrite.
