Science Museum Lates Exhibition 26 June 2019

Exhibition in collaboration with evolutionary biologist Dr Simone Immler from University of East Anglia, about her research https://simoneimmler.com/

We are exhibiting a new body of work comprised off a virtual reality artwork and a sculpture.

Find us at the stand: Fertilisation, a new beginning?

Find out how sperm and eggs transfer important information from parents to offspring – and learn why fertilisation isn't necessarily a new beginning, but a key step in the circle of life.’
