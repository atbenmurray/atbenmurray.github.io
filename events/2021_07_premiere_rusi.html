Phenotypica Premiere of Latest AI Artwork and Discussion

1st July 2021, 12:30h (BST)

Our first artwork about artificial intelligence, commissioned by Ali Hossaini from The National Gallery X and RUSI will be shown for the first time this Thursday 1 July 2021 at 12:30h (BST) at the virtual conference 'Trusting Machines? Cross-sector Lessons from Healthcare and Security'.

We'll also have a short discussion about AI and the process of making the artwork with Ali Hossaini. It'll be a great opportunity to ask us questions!

More information about the conference and free tickets here https://www.tas.ac.uk/bigeventscpt/trusting-machines/
